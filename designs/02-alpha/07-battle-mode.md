# Battle Mode

Regular pong but against the rival instead of with a partner. Both sides have items and effects. No special rules, no different objectives. Just pong against someone who's trying to win.

---

## Pre-break

The rival appears mid pre-break and gates key milestones through battle mode. To pass a milestone, the player has to beat the rival. The rival's energy reads as sports rivalry on the surface: the reckless, competitive friend who never let the main character coast.

---

## Post-break

The rival is gone. Battle mode is gone with them. The badge challenges use the same underlying mode (ball and paddle, opponent) but the opponent and conditions vary based on the narrative item placed in the badge. Some challenges may have modified conditions tied to the item's narrative weight.

---

## Post-game

In Peace, battle mode is freely available. The rival is a partner and a training opponent. No stakes, no gates.
